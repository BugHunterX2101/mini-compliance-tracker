# Design Document: Mini Compliance Tracker 3D ## 1. Vision & Strategy The Mini Compliance Tracker is designed to provide compliance teams at LedgersCFO with a premium, high-performance workspace. The "Architectural Ledger" strategy moves away from flat design, utilizing 3D depth, glassmorphism, and fluid micro-animations to create a sense of trust, institutional reliability, and modern efficiency. ## 2. Visual Identity - **Color Palette:** Deep slates (#0F172A), crisp whites, and vibrant compliance-status accents (Success Green, Critical Red, In-Progress Blue). - **Typography:** Inter — chosen for its high legibility and architectural feel in financial contexts. - **Surface Strategy:** Layered glassmorphism with backdrop blurs and subtle 3D tilt transforms to provide tactile feedback and visual hierarchy. ## 3. Interaction Principles - **3D Depth:** Elements react to user focus with subtle Z-axis shifts. - **Fluidity:** All state transitions (modals, progress updates) utilize spring-based animations for a world-class feel. - **Responsiveness:** Designed first for mobile efficiency, ensuring compliance officers can track filings on the go. --- *Created for LedgersCFO — March 2026*
